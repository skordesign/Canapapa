== Description ==
Woocommerce Best Selling Products side bar Widget Shows the No of Heighest sell Product On the front end side bar and Get ‘Best Selling Products’ Widget & Optimize Sales .
Display the best selling products on your WooCommerce website with this easy-to-install plugin by Helios Solutions. The widget is a WooCommerce Add-On that offers simple features in order to highlight and aesthetically represent your best selling products on your webshop.
Thought & Developed by Ecommerce Development Experts @HeliosSolutions

The widget will feature in the backend of your website dashboard. You can customize the list of products as per your requirements to sell more through telling your visitors about the best selling products on your platform. 


= Features =

-Woo commerce Drag & Drop Widget  
-Easily customizable using the widget option
-Displaying number of product setting




== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

e.g.

1. Upload the plugin folder `Woocommerce Best Selling Products widget` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins -> Installed Plugins -> Activate Woocommerce Best Selling Products widget" menu in WordPress.
3. Find Woocommerce Best Selling Products widget plugin ,which is located From Appearance menu -> Widgets -> Woocommerce Best Selling Products widget.
